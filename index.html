Your Peace of Mind

Privacy, by design.

We believe your space is your space. Here's exactly what we have — and don't have — so you can stay with full confidence.

✅

Your room is 100% private

No cameras, no monitoring devices, and no smart speakers inside any guest room or bathroom. What happens in your room stays in your room.

🔒

Your door locks

Every guest room has a keyed door lock. Only you hold the key during your stay. Neither the host nor other guests can enter without your permission.

📷

Exterior cameras

There is one security camera on the exterior of the property facing the front entrance, for the safety of all guests. It covers the front door only — never interior spaces.

📵

No noise monitoring

We do not use noise monitoring devices inside the home. We trust our guests and prefer to set clear expectations upfront rather than surveil.

🗂️

Your data

We only collect what Airbnb provides us to facilitate your stay. We never share, sell, or store your personal information beyond what's required to host you.

🤝

Fellow guests

Other guests in the home are also verified Airbnb guests. We never share your personal details — name, contact, or booking info — with other guests.

Life in Capitol Hill

The city doesn't sleep.
You still will.

Capitol Hill is one of Seattle's most vibrant neighborhoods — and one of its loudest. Here's what to expect and how to make your nights restful.

🚗

Street noise

15th Ave E is a busy arterial. You'll hear traffic throughout the day and into the evening — buses, cars, and the occasional siren are part of the Capitol Hill soundtrack.

🎶

Nightlife

Bars, music venues, and restaurants on Pike/Pine are a short walk away. Friday and Saturday nights bring extra energy to the neighborhood — typically winding down around 2 AM.

🚑

Emergency vehicles

Swedish Medical Center is nearby, which means sirens are a regular occurrence day and night. This is normal for the area and not cause for alarm.

🤍

White noise machine

Every room is equipped with a white noise machine on the bedside table. It's the single most effective thing for sleeping through city noise — we highly recommend turning it on, even if you don't think you'll need it.

How to use Turn the dial clockwise to increase volume. Start at the midpoint — enough to mask street noise without being distracting. The fan setting works best for most guests.
🪟

Windows

Keeping your window closed significantly reduces outside noise. Both rooms have blackout curtains which also provide an extra layer of sound dampening.

🎧

Still can't sleep?

Earplugs are available in the top drawer of your bedside table. If the noise is unusually disruptive, don't hesitate to reach out — we're here to help.

How Things Work

House Manual.

Quick guides for everything in the home. Each section has its own QR-friendly link — scan to jump straight to what you need.

01
🔐

Door Lock

  1. Enter the 4-digit code 4829 on the lockbox to retrieve the key on arrival.
  2. Insert key and turn clockwise to unlock, counter-clockwise to lock.
  3. Always lock your room door when leaving — even briefly.
  4. On departure, leave the key on the kitchen counter.

Lost your key? Call or text us immediately at +1 (555) 000-0000.

02
🍳

Oven & Stovetop

  1. Turn the left knob to set oven temperature (350°F is standard baking).
  2. Allow 10–15 minutes for the oven to preheat.
  3. For stovetop, turn the individual burner knob — the igniter clicks automatically.
  4. Always turn all knobs back to OFF when done.

If a burner won't ignite, hold the knob down for 5 seconds while it clicks.

03
📡

Microwave

  1. Place food inside and close the door firmly.
  2. Press the number keys to set time (e.g. 1, 0, 0 for 1 minute).
  3. Press Start.
  4. Press Stop/Clear at any time to pause or cancel.

Never microwave metal containers, foil, or sealed containers.

04
❄️

AC & Heat

  1. The thermostat is on the wall near the room entrance.
  2. Press the Mode button to toggle between Cool, Heat, and Fan.
  3. Use the ▲ ▼ arrows to set your desired temperature.
  4. The system takes 5–10 minutes to reach the set temperature.

Please keep windows closed while AC or heat is running for efficiency.
